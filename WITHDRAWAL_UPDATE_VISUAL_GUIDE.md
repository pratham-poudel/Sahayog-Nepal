utilized for the campaign's stated purpose.

## What Donors/Viewers Will See

### ✅ Transparent Information:
- **Date** of withdrawal completion
- **Exact amount** withdrawn
- **Processing fees** (if any)
- **Final amount** transferred to creator
- **Purpose statement** for transparency

### ❌ Privacy Protected:
- NO bank account numbers
- NO transaction reference IDs
- NO personal banking details
- NO admin comments (internal only)
