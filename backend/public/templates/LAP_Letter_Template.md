# Local Authority Permission (LAP) Letter Template

## Format for Local Authority Permission Letter

**Date:** [Date]

**To,**  
The Administrator/CEO  
Sahayog Nepal  
[Address]

**Subject: Request for Permission to Start Fundraising Campaign**

**Respected Sir/Madam,**

I, **[Your Full Name]**, a resident of **[Your Complete Address]**, Ward No. **[Ward Number]**, **[Municipality/Village]**, **[District]**, hereby request permission to start a fundraising campaign on Sahayog Nepal platform.

### Campaign Details:
- **Campaign Title:** [Your Campaign Title]
- **Purpose:** [Brief description of the purpose - Medical Treatment/Education/Emergency/Community Project etc.]
- **Target Amount:** NPR [Amount]
- **Duration:** [Start Date] to [End Date]

### Background:
[Provide brief background about why this fundraising is needed. Include relevant details about the beneficiary, medical condition, educational needs, or community project requirements]

### Purpose of Funds:
The funds raised through this campaign will be utilized for:
1. [Specific use 1]
2. [Specific use 2]
3. [Specific use 3]

I hereby confirm that:
- All information provided is true and accurate to the best of my knowledge
- The funds raised will be used solely for the stated purpose
- I will provide regular updates about the campaign progress
- I will comply with all terms and conditions of Sahayog Nepal platform

**Applicant's Details:**
- **Full Name:** [Your Full Name]
- **Contact Number:** [Your Phone Number]
- **Email:** [Your Email]
- **Citizenship Number:** [Your Citizenship Number]
- **Address:** [Your Complete Address]

**Attachments:**
1. Copy of Citizenship/Passport
2. Supporting Documents (Medical Reports/Educational Documents/Project Proposal etc.)
3. [Any other relevant documents]

---

**Yours Sincerely,**

[Your Signature]  
[Your Full Name]  
[Date]

## Instructions:
1. Fill in all the details marked with [brackets]
2. Get the letter signed by your local authority (Ward Office/Municipality/VDC)
3. Ensure the official seal/stamp is clearly visible
4. Scan or take a clear photo of the signed and stamped letter
5. Upload the document in PDF or image format (JPG, PNG)

## Important Notes:
- The letter must be on official letterhead or contain official seal/stamp
- All information must be accurate and verifiable
- False information may lead to campaign rejection or legal action
- Keep original documents for future reference

For any queries, contact: support@sahayognepal.com
