## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### Step 1: Login
1. Navigate to `/employee` portal
2. Select **"Legal & Compliance"** department
3. Enter your **Designation Number** (e.g., LEGAL001)
4. Enter your registered **Phone Number**
5. Enter your 5-digit **Access Code**
6. Click **"Send OTP"**
7. Enter the 6-digit OTP sent to your phone
8. Click **"Verify & Login"**

### Step 2: Dashboard Overview
Once logged in, you'll see:
- **Total Alerts**: All alerts in the system
- **Pending Review**: Alerts awaiting your review
- **Total Reviewed**: Completed reviews
- **Reports Filed**: STR/TTR reports submitted

### Step 3: Filtering Alerts
Use the filter dropdowns to narrow down alerts:
- **Review Status**: All/Unreviewed/Reviewed
- **Outcome**: All/Pending/Under Review/Reported/Dismissed
- **Risk Level**: All/High Risk/Medium Risk/Low Risk

### Step 4: Searching
Type in the search box to find:
- User names or emails
- Campaign titles
- Transaction references
- Risk indicators

### Step 5: Reviewing an Alert
1. Click on any alert row to open details
2. Review:
   - Risk Score & Indicators
   - User Information & KYC Status
   - Transaction/Donation Details
   - Campaign Information
3. Select Review Outcome:
   - **Reported**: File an official report (requires STR or TTR type)
   - **Under Review**: Need more investigation
   - **Dismissed**: False positive/no action needed
4. Add optional notes
5. Click **"Submit Review"**

### When to Report
**File STR if**:
- Multiple high-risk indicators present
- Pattern suggests money laundering
- User behavior is suspicious
- Transaction structure is unusual
- KYC information is questionable

**File TTR if**:
- Transaction exceeds regulatory threshold
- Required by compliance rules
- Multiple large transactions detected

### When to Dismiss
**Dismiss if**:
- False positive confirmed
- User is verified and legitimate
- Transaction is explainable
- No suspicious patterns found
- Single low-risk indicator only
