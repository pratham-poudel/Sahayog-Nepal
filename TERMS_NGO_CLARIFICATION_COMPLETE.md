## Key Principles Established

### 1. **NGO Takes Full Responsibility**
Once a campaign is verified and published, it becomes an official NGO campaign with full NGO backing.

### 2. **Strict Verification Before Disbursement**
Multiple layers of verification including:
- Document checks
- Physical verification
- NGO representative presence for large amounts
- Video/photo documentation
- Signed receipts

### 3. **Zero Tolerance for Fraud**
Any detected fraud results in:
- Immediate fund freeze
- Investigation
- Transfer to DHUKUTI (if fraud confirmed)
- Permanent ban
- Legal action
- Donor notification

### 4. **Funds Always Serve Charity**
Even fraudulent campaign funds are redirected to DHUKUTI to ensure they still serve genuine charitable purposes.

### 5. **Pure Charitable Model**
No financial returns, no equity, no interest - only pure charitable giving.
