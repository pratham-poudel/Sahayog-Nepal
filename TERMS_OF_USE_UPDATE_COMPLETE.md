- NGO's charitable activities

This ensures efficient utilization of unutilized funds and maintains transparency.

## Legal Compliance Notes

### Why These Changes Matter:

1. **Regulatory Compliance:**
   - Nepal currently lacks specific crowdfunding regulations
   - Operating as registered NGO ensures legal compliance under Social Welfare Act
   - Aligns with charitable organization norms

2. **1-Year Withdrawal Policy Rationale:**
   - Prevents fund stagnation and ensures efficient resource utilization
   - Aligns with international best practices for charitable crowdfunding
   - Provides clear timeline for campaigners
   - Ensures funds ultimately serve charitable purposes

3. **DHUKUTI Fund:**
   - Central charitable fund for NGO
   - Receives unutilized campaign funds after 1-year deadline
   - Redistributed to active needy campaigns
   - Subject to NGO financial reporting and transparency requirements

4. **Dual Structure Benefits:**
   - Sahayog Nepal (NGO): Legal entity, fund management, charitable activities
   - Dallytech Pvt Ltd: Technology infrastructure, platform maintenance
   - Separation ensures compliance while maintaining technical excellence
